Alps Expedition & Adventures #1

Overall Information:

  • Country: Austria - Italy
  • Trip date: 07.01.2024 - 13.01.2024
  • Perfect for: Intermediates
  • Difficulty level: Advanced
  • Age range: +21
  • Duration: 6 Nights, 7 Days
  • Number of participants: 6-8
  • Cost: 499$
  • Trip start time: Zurich Airport on 07.01.2024 at 12:00.
  • Trip end time: Zurich Airport on 13.01.2024 at 18:00

Embark on a 7-10 day intermediate-level mountain climbing and hiking adventure in the Austrian and Italian Alps. Traverse scenic trails, summit peaks like Austria's Dachstein or Italy's Tre Cime di Lavaredo, and explore charming villages for a unique blend of cultures. Ensure good fitness, pack essentials, navigate with maps or GPS, and be prepared for variable mountain weather.

Day 01: "Alpine Arrival"

Arrive at the picturesque starting point, possibly a charming alpine village. Get acquainted with fellow adventurers and enjoy a welcome dinner introducing the unique local cuisine.

Day 02: "Village Exploration and Trail Kickoff"

Explore the alpine village, absorbing the cultural nuances. Begin the hiking journey with a trail that provides stunning views of the surrounding landscapes. Reach a cozy mountain hut for the night.

Day 03: "Summit Ascent - Conquering Dachstein"

Embark on a challenging ascent to the intermediate-level peak of Dachstein. Marvel at the panoramic vistas from the summit. Descend to a different mountain hut for an evening of camaraderie and local mountain delicacies.

Day 04: "Alpine Lakes and Wildlife Encounter"

Traverse trails leading to crystal-clear alpine lakes. Take in the serenity and, with luck, encounter alpine wildlife. Reach another mountain hut nestled near the lakes for a night of relaxation.

Day 05: "Hut-to-Hut Adventure"

Experience the traditional alpine practice of hut-to-hut hiking. Journey through diverse terrains, with each hut offering unique views and a taste of local hospitality. Enjoy an evening of stories and shared experiences.

Day 06: "Cable Car Extravaganza and Cultural Immersion"

Opt for a cable car excursion to access higher altitudes, easing the day's journey. Immerse yourself in cultural activities, perhaps visiting historical sites or engaging with the local community. Conclude the day at a mountain hut with a farewell celebration.

Day 07: "Scenic Descent and Departure"

Descend through scenic trails, reflecting on the week's adventures. Arrive back at the starting point, bid farewell to newfound friends, and depart with memories of a remarkable alpine experience.
